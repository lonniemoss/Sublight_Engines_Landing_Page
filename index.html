THE PERFECTION IN SECURITY

Did you know that elevators are one of the most secure forms of transportation? That's because they're designed to be as safe as possible, with multiple fail-safes in place to prevent accidents.

Elevators are equipped with a variety of safety features, including:

- Brakes that stop the elevator if it starts to fall

- Doors that open automatically in an emergency

- Emergency stop buttons inside the elevator

- Alarms that sound if the elevator doors are opened without authorization

- Cameras that monitor activity .
